Privacy Policy

How UniTrade collects, uses, and protects your information

Effective: April 3, 2026
Your privacy matters. UniTrade collects only what's necessary to run the marketplace. Your Student ID is stored securely and is never shared with buyers or other students. We do not sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account data (when you register)

  • Full name and email address
  • Student ID — used solely to verify LUANAR enrollment
  • Campus, phone number
  • Password (stored as a one-way hash — we cannot read it)

Listing and activity data

  • Listings you post (title, description, photos, price, category)
  • Comments and replies you make
  • Items you like or save
  • Payment method and transaction reference you provide

Automatically collected

  • Session tokens (stored in your browser, expire on logout)
  • Approximate activity timestamps for listings and comments

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify you are a current LUANAR student
  • To display your listings and profile on the marketplace
  • To send notifications about your listings (likes, comments, replies)
  • To process and verify listing fee payments
  • To enforce our Terms & Conditions and prevent abuse

3. What We Share

  • With buyers: your display name, campus, and phone number (if you enable it in privacy settings)
  • With university administration: only if required for a formal dispute or disciplinary process
  • We never sell your data to advertisers or third parties
  • Your Student ID is never visible to other users

4. Data Security

  • Passwords are hashed with SHA-256 and a unique salt — never stored as plain text
  • Session tokens are randomly generated and expire on logout
  • The database is stored server-side and not accessible to other users
  • Photos are stored as base64 data within the database

5. Your Rights

  • View your data: available in your Profile page
  • Edit your data: update name, contact, bio anytime in Profile → Account
  • Delete your account: Profile → Security → Delete Account — permanently removes all your data
  • Request data export: email support@unitrade.luanar.ac.mw

6. Cookies & Local Storage

UniTrade uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store your authentication token and user details. This data stays on your device and is cleared when you sign out. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via platform notification. Continued use constitutes acceptance. Governed by the laws of Malawi.

8. Contact

Questions about your privacy: support@unitrade.luanar.ac.mw — Innovation Hub, LUANAR Main Campus, Lilongwe.
