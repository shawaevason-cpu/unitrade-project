UniTrade is revolutionizing how university students buy, sell, and connect on campus. A better way to buy and sell on campus.

Our Mission

Every semester, thousands of students struggle to sell their goods and services, or connect with trustworthy buyers. We experienced this frustration firsthand.

UniTrade was born at LUANAR, created by a student who noted that more students were lacking the best way to advertise their goods and services.
