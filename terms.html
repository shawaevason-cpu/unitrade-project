Rules for using UniTrade Marketplace

Effective Date: April 3, 2026
Summary: Be honest, be safe, be respectful. UniTrade is a community marketplace for verified LUANAR students. By using our platform, you agree to these terms. Violations may result in account suspension or permanent ban.

1. Eligibility & Account

1.1 Who Can Use UniTrade

  • Currently enrolled students at LUANAR from all campuses
  • Your Student ID will be used only to recognise you as a current student and is stored securely
  • Must provide a valid email address for verification
  • One account per person — duplicate accounts are prohibited

1.2 Account Responsibilities

  • Maintain accurate profile information at all times
  • Keep your password secure and do not share it
  • You are responsible for all activity under your account
  • Report unauthorised access immediately via the Report page

2. Listings & Transactions

2.1 Posting Rules

  • All listings must include accurate descriptions and real photos of the actual item
  • Prices must be stated in Malawian Kwacha (MK)
  • No misleading titles, clickbait, or exaggerated claims
  • Listings expire automatically after 30 days and can be renewed for an additional fee
  • Listing fee is a percentage of your sale price (see Fee Schedule below) — non-refundable
⚠️ Strictly Prohibited Items — Selling these will result in immediate ban and possible referral to university administration or legal action:
  • Counterfeit goods
  • Stolen property
  • Weapons or explosives
  • Drugs or paraphernalia
  • Adult content/services
  • Academic cheating services
  • Personal information of others
  • Hazardous materials

2.2 Transaction Guidelines

  • All negotiations must happen through UniTrade (comments) or WhatsApp
  • Cash transactions are at your own risk — meet in safe, public locations on campus
  • UniTrade is not responsible for transaction disputes between users
  • No off-platform payment schemes to circumvent listing fees

3. User Conduct

  • No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech in any form
  • No spamming or excessive messaging to other users
  • No creation of fake listings or fake accounts
  • No intentionally misleading buyers about item condition or authenticity
  • No no-shows without prior communication with the other party
  • No attempts to circumvent platform fees
  • No sharing of other users' private or contact information
  • Be respectful and professional in all communications
  • No profanity or offensive language in listings or comments
  • No external links in listing descriptions (except verified partners)

5. Safety & Liability

Always: Meet in public campus locations, bring a friend, inspect items before paying, and trust your instincts. Never share banking PINs or passwords.
  • UniTrade provides safety guidelines but is not liable for in-person meetings or transactions
  • Report suspicious behaviour immediately using the Report page
  • Use WhatsApp voice/video call to verify identity before meeting a stranger
  • UniTrade is a marketplace platform — not a chatting platform
  • We do not guarantee item quality, authenticity, or seller reliability
  • Users are responsible for verifying items before purchasing

6. Content & Intellectual Property

  • You retain ownership of content (photos, descriptions) you post
  • You grant UniTrade a licence to display your content on the platform
  • Do not post copyrighted material (music, film, software) without permission
  • UniTrade logo and branding are protected — do not reproduce without permission

7. Account Termination

7.1 We May Suspend or Ban You For:

  • Violating any section of these terms
  • Fraudulent, deceptive, or illegal activity
  • Multiple verified complaints from other users
  • No longer being an enrolled student at LUANAR

7.2 Effect of Termination

  • All active listings will be removed immediately
  • Outstanding fees remain due
  • You may not create new accounts after a ban
  • Certain data is retained for legal compliance per Malawian data laws

8. Dispute Resolution

  • First, try to resolve disputes directly with the other party via WhatsApp or comments
  • Unresolved disputes may be escalated to university administration (LUANAR Student Affairs)
  • Legal disputes are governed by the laws of Malawi, jurisdiction of Lilongwe Courts

9. Changes to Terms

We may update these terms. Material changes will be notified via platform notifications and email at least 7 days before taking effect. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
